Director's Message

Elite group of colleges has been continuing it’s great consistency since 32 years in educating the youth to show up their potential on the national and international level. We aim to extract the competent youngsters and provide them with a chance to achieve their goals under renowned research oriented faculty. It will be a great honor for me to mention here the alumni of Elite working nationwide in distinguish places. The goal is to serve the society, to make the economy grow and to list Pakistan in the prosperous countries of the world. The brave students of Elite are capable to create a drastic positive change in the country.

We as a nation need competent people in the health, economic and the research sector of our country and Elite group of Colleges feels proud in saying that it’s huge number of proficient students are serving in numerous areas of the country.

Motivational sessions are held in the college in order to boost up the confidence level of the students. We aim at developing students holistically not only in securing excellent grades but also in grooming their personalities as responsible citizens of Pakistan. The idea is to prepare the students for the practical hardships also. The campuses are equipped with latest lab equipment and libraries. Moreover thousands of scholarships are been provided to students. We together can bring a change and for this purpose I welcome you to Elite group of colleges and ensure you a great and prosperous journey with us.

Imtiaz Ahmed
